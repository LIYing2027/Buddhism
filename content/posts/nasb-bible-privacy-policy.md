This Privacy Policy applies to our **Bible** apps.

Our Bible apps are designed to help users read and study the Bible on their device.

Your privacy is important to us. We do not collect, store, sell, or share your personal information.

<!--more-->

## Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect personal information from users.

This includes, but is not limited to:

- Your name
- Email address
- Phone number
- Home address
- Contacts
- Photos
- Calendar information
- Personal documents
- Bible reading history
- Bookmarks, notes, or reading preferences

Any reading activity, bookmarks, notes, or settings used inside the app are intended only to support the app experience. We do not sell or disclose this information to third parties.

## Advertising

The app may display ads through third-party advertising services such as Google AdMob.

Advertising providers may process certain device or usage information in order to provide, measure, and improve ads. This may include information such as device identifiers, IP address, app interactions, approximate location, or advertising-related data, depending on your device settings and consent choices.

We do not provide advertisers with your personal information, Bible reading history, bookmarks, notes, or private app content.

We do not sell your personal information. Any information processed by advertising providers is handled according to their own privacy policies.

## Third-Party Services

The app may use third-party services for advertising, analytics, crash reporting, or app functionality.

These services may collect or process information according to their own privacy policies. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party services, websites, or links.

Examples of third-party services may include:

- Google AdMob
- Google Play services
- Apple services

## Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect user privacy and keep the app experience safe.

Please keep your device, Apple ID, Google account, and app store accounts secure.

## Children's Privacy

Our Bible apps do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

If you believe that a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us and we will take appropriate action.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make important changes, we will update this page with the latest version.

Your continued use of the app after changes are posted means you accept the updated Privacy Policy.
