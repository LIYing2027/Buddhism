This Privacy Policy applies to the app **Cloud Music Offline Listening**.

Cloud Music Offline Listening is a music player app that allows users to import audio files from cloud storage services, WiFi transfer, iTunes File Sharing, iCloud, and local files, then play and manage those music files offline inside the app.

Your privacy is important to us. We designed this app to work mainly on your device, and we do not collect personal information from users.

<!--more-->

## Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, store, sell, or share your personal information.

This includes, but is not limited to:

- Your name
- Email address
- Phone number
- Home address
- Contacts
- Photos
- Calendar information
- Personal documents
- Music files imported into the app
- Playlists created inside the app
- Cloud storage account passwords

Your imported music files and playlists are used only for playback and management inside the app. We do not upload your music files to our own servers.

## Cloud Storage Access

Cloud Music Offline Listening may allow you to connect to third-party cloud storage services such as Google Drive, Dropbox, OneDrive, or iCloud.

When you choose to connect a cloud service, the connection is used only to let you browse, import, and download your own music files into the app.

We do not receive or store your cloud account password. Authentication is handled by the relevant cloud service provider. Your use of those services is also subject to their own privacy policies and terms.

## Local Music and Offline Playback

Music files imported into the app are stored locally on your device so that you can play them offline.

We do not monitor the content of your music files. We do not analyze your music library for advertising, tracking, or profiling purposes.

## WiFi Transfer and File Import

If you use WiFi transfer, iTunes File Sharing, iCloud, or other file import methods, the files you import are used only for local playback and playlist management.

We do not collect or transmit these files to our servers.

## Non-Personal Information

The app may process limited technical information necessary for normal app operation, such as app settings, playback status, playlist structure, or local file information.

This information is used only to provide app features and improve the user experience. It is not used to personally identify you.

## Advertising

The app may display ads through third-party advertising services such as Google AdMob.

Advertising providers may collect or process certain device or usage information in order to provide, measure, and improve ads. This may include information such as device identifiers, IP address, app interactions, approximate location, or advertising-related data, depending on your device settings and consent choices.

We do not sell your personal information. Any data collected by advertising providers is handled according to their own privacy policies.

You can learn more about how Google uses information for advertising from Google’s privacy and advertising policies.

## Third-Party Services

The app may use third-party services for cloud storage access, advertising, analytics, or app functionality.

These services may collect information according to their own privacy policies. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party services, websites, or links.

Examples of third-party services may include:

- Google Drive
- Dropbox
- OneDrive
- iCloud
- Google AdMob

## Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect user privacy and keep the app experience safe.

Because the app is designed mainly for local music playback, your imported music files and playlists remain on your device unless you choose to use external cloud services or transfer methods.

Please keep your device, Apple ID, and cloud storage accounts secure.

## Children’s Privacy

Cloud Music Offline Listening does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

If you believe that a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us and we will take appropriate action.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make important changes, we will update this page with the latest version.

Your continued use of the app after changes are posted means you accept the updated Privacy Policy.
